Misinformation & Hate Speech

A common misinformation about social media is that everything posted on it is true. However, social media can be a breeding ground for fake news and misinformation. Anyone can post without fact-checking, and algorithms prioritize sensational content.


Some people intentionally spread false information to serve their own interests. It's crucial to fact-check and be critical of what you see on social media.
